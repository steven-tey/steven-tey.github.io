About Me


Hey there! My name is Steven Tey. Originally from Malaysia, I'm currently in my senior year at Minerva, where I major in data science and brand management.

I am passionate about building products that make a positive impact on people's lives.

When I was 19, I published a book that sold over 1.5K copies nationwide and went on to win various international and national awards. It now has over 850K reads on Wattpad.

This summer (2020), I built a SaaS that got to #2 on Product Hunt and the front page of Hacker News. It currently has 3K MAU and is generating around $3.5K in MRR, with a churn rate of just below 5%. If you're interested, here's a Hackernoon article detailing my experience.

In the past, I've been the product owner at various tech startups in New York, Berlin, and Tokyo, specifically in the EdTech, HealthTech and blockchain industries.

Other things I love include running, music, filmmaking, and reading (I'm aiming to read at least one book every month, and for this month it's Ray Dalio's Principles).

I'm always open to exciting new projects, so if you are currently working on one, feel free to reach out on Twitter or via email.
